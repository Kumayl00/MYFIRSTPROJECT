Support

Find links to mental health professionals and hotlines available for immediate help.

  • Samaritans

    24/7 emotional support for anyone struggling to cope or feeling distressed.

    Call: 116 123 (Free, 24/7)
    Visit Samaritans Website
  • Mind

    Provides advice and support for mental health issues, including anxiety and depression.

    Call: 0300 123 3393 (Mon-Fri, 9 am to 6 pm)
    Visit Mind Website
  • Shout

    A free 24/7 crisis text line for anyone in the UK.

    Text: 85258 (Free, 24/7)
    Visit Shout Website
  • Rethink Mental Illness

    Support and information for people affected by mental illness.

    Call: 0808 801 0411 (Mon-Fri, 9:30 am to 4 pm)
    Visit Rethink Website
